# Pollination
#biology/plants #NCERT/12 

The transfer of [[Pollen]] grains from [[Anther]] to the [[Stigma]] of a [[Pistil]]

### Types of Pollination
- [[Autogamy]] ![[Autogamy]]
- [[Geitonogamy]] ![[Geitonogamy]]
- [[Xenogamy]] ![[Xenogamy]]

### Pollinating Agents
#### Abiotic
- Air - [[Anemophily]]
- Water - [[Hydrophily]]
#### Biotic (more common)
- Animals - Zoophily
	- Insects - [[Entomophily]]
	- Birds - [[Ornithophily]]
	- Snakes
	- Slugs
	- Bats
