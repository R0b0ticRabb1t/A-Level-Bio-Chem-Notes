## Telophase
#biology 

Final part of [[Mitosis]].
- 2 [[Nucleus]] reforms
- [[Nucleolus]] reforms for each nucleus
- Spindle fibres breakdown
- [[Chromatids]] uncoil again after reaching the poles
[[Cytokinesis]] comes next
