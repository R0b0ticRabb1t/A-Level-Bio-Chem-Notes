## Phrophase
#biology 

First part of [[Mitosis]].

- [[Chromosomes]] become more visible as  [[Chromatin]] coils up.
Late prophase
- [[Nuclear Envelope]] disappears
- [[Nucleolus]] disappears
- Duplicated [[Centrosome]] move to poles of the cell
