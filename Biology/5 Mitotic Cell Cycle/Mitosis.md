# Mitosis
#biology

This is a process of cell division.
The M phase of the [[5 Mitotic Cell Cycle]] is when the nucleus divides.

Contains three parts:
- [[Prophase]]
- [[Metaphase]]
- [[Anaphase]]
- [[Telophase]]
