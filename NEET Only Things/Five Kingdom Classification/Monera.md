#biology #NCERT 

basically just [[Bacteria]], monera is all [[Prokaryotic]] organisms
- most abundant 
- found in extreme habitats
- will outlive us all
- extensive metabolic diversity
	- some can do [[photosynthesis]]
	- [[chemosynthetic]]
	- but most [[Heterotrophic]]
